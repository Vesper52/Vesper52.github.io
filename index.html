Summary of Project

The purpose of this project was to analyze various weather paramters within cities as you get closer to the equator. We gathered the data from the OpenWeatherMap AI to get a dataset of over 500 cities.

We then used Python and Matplotlib to plot different parameters (Humidity,Temperature,Cloudiness,and Wind Speed) against latitude. This site gives a quick look through all these graphs as well as brief explanations of the data.
